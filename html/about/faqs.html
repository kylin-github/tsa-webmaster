Frequently Asked Question

  • What is a hackathon?
    add_circle remove_circle
    It can be whatever you want! Individuals or teams work togehter to code a product in a sprint-type competition. It's a bit of a code-fest, and during this hackathon, you'll have theopportunity to learn plenty of things and meet new people along the way!
  • When is the hackathon?
    add_circle remove_circle
    The hackathon runs from April 8 to April 10, 2022. For more information, check out the "Timeline"
  • Who can participate in the hackathon?
    add_circle remove_circle
    Anyone is welcome! No matter what level of experience you have, we'll be sure to provide you with a valuable opportunity!
  • What can I win?
    add_circle remove_circle
    There's plenty of swag to win, including our very own TechTV subcription and food from Krusty Kitchen! To learn more, click here.
  • Do I need an idea already?
    add_circle remove_circle
    No! There will be time for you and your team to brainstorm, and we will provide resources and support to help you. With that said, if you have an idea already, even better! We'll help you expand on and refine your idea during the hackathon.
  • Do I need a team already?
    add_circle remove_circle
    No! Individuals are welcome to sign up and find a team through the team building event at the beginning of the hackathon.
